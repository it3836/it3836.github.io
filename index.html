Pineapple Belongs on Pizza

Welcome! This website presents a constructive and thoughtful argument for why pineapple deserves a place on pizza. More than a fleeting trend, the inclusion of pineapple challenges culinary boundaries and celebrates the fusion of flavors from around the world.

A Brief History

Pizza has a long and storied history, originating in Italy as a humble street food before evolving into a global culinary icon. In the 1960s, a creative chef in Canada dared to add pineapple to pizza, mixing the sweetness of tropical fruit with savory ingredients. This bold innovation sparked both admiration and controversy, igniting debates that persist to this day.

Cultural Impact

The debate over pineapple on pizza goes beyond taste—it represents the dynamic evolution of food culture. Embracing pineapple on pizza is an acknowledgment that culinary traditions are not static but are continually reshaped by cultural influences and innovative ideas.

Embracing Innovation

Adding pineapple to pizza is a celebration of creativity in the culinary arts. It reminds us that food can be a canvas for experimentation, where blending unexpected flavors can lead to delightful discoveries. This spirit of innovation has enriched our gastronomic landscape, inviting both chefs and food enthusiasts to explore new horizons.
